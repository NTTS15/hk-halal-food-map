# HK Halal Food Map (伊路同行)

A digital navigation solution designed to bridge the information gap for the Muslim community and tourists in Hong Kong. This project was developed for the **3rd "Smart Consumer Academy" (第參屆 智醒消費學堂)** competition to promote social inclusion and responsible consumption.

## 🌟 Overview
Despite the growing Muslim population in Hong Kong, finding reliable Halal dining options remains a challenge. Our research found that **67.3% of Muslims prefer cooking at home** due to the difficulty of locating certified restaurants. This tool aims to lower that barrier and foster a "Muslim-friendly" urban environment.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Verified Data**: Built using the official [IUHK Halal List](https://www.iuhk.org/images/Others/Halah-Food/Halal-List_en.pdf) to ensure data reliability.
* **Visual Trust Grading**: Color-coded markers distinguish between "Halal Certified Restaurants," "Halal Kitchens," and "Halal-Friendly" spots.
* **District Filtering**: Quickly browse by area (e.g., Tsim Sha Tsui, Wan Chai) to find the nearest options.
* **SDG Alignment**: Supports **SDG 10 (Reduced Inequalities)** and **SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production)**.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
* **Assistant**: Developed with the assistance of **Claude Sonnet (4.6)**.
* **Mapping**: Powered by **Google Maps API** for real-time navigation and precise geocoding.
* **Future Scope**: Scalable to include other dietary restrictions such as Vegan, Kosher, or common food allergens.

## 🚀 Impact
By reducing the time and anxiety associated with finding Halal food, this project encourages outdoor consumption, supports local businesses, and enhances Hong Kong's reputation as a diverse and inclusive international hub.
